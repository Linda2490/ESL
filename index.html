How it works

Key Features

With a click of a button in monday.com, set the item you wish to work on within Outlook.

Create new items and subitems from outlook

Save your most used boards, groups, items and subitems by marking them as favorites

Email updates:detailed explanation

Add the monday.com for Outlook add-in

Go To our Microsoft AppSource page: monday.com for Outlook Or just download it straight from your Outlook by clicking ‘Get add-ins’ and search for ‘monday’.

Click ‘Get it now’.

Open a new email window (or forward/reply to an existing email), and click on the Monday.com add-in.

You should be prompted to authenticate your monday.com account with your monday.com login credentials and by clicking ‘connect to monday’

Start working with the add-in

Add one of our integration recipes, for example: "when a Button is clicked, set this item as current working item in outlook", to your selected board in monday.com

Select an item to work on by clicking the button we just created

When you open the outlook add-in, the item you selected with the button will be selected for you automatically

Frequently Asked Questions

    How to create a new item ?

    If you wish to create a new item, simply click the ‘create new item’ option, and give it a name.

    This will create a new item in the group selected above and set it automatically as your current working item.

    If you wish to use your email's subject as your item's name, you can do so by clicking on the 'Copy email's subject' checkbox.

    How to get to frequently used Items by using Favorites ?

    You can choose your favorite Boards, Groups, Items and Subitems, simply click on the grey star, when it turns to gold it is marked as a favorite.

    Next time you will open the Outlook add-in, these will be available at the top.

    How to update a monday item by email from the Outlook ?

    Select your board, group & item you wish to view, edit, or add an update to. (This can be done directly from monday as described above)

    Select 'Bcc', 'Cc' or 'To' in the email options and click ‘Add Item to Bcc'.

    Finish editing your email and press 'Send', your monday.com item will be automatically updated with the email subject, body & attachments.

    How to update a monday item's column values from the Outlook add-in ?

    You can edit each Item or Sub-Item by clicking the 'View item card' button.

    Here you can edit all the item's columns (that are currently supported by the monday API) and the Item's name.

    Click on the field that you would like to update, and A dropdown will open with all the available options.

    How to save last state ?

    The last item you worked on will be saved automatically for later use within the Outlook add-in, you can change this behavior from the 'Settings' menu.
